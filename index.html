About Dukaan

At dukaan, our mission is to provide a sustainable and affordable solution for buying and selling quality used items. We believe in reducing waste and promoting a circular economy, which is why we've created a platform that connects buyers and sellers of pre-owned goods. We have a dedicated team of professionals who are passionate about sustainability and customer service. Our team works hard to ensure that our platform is safe, reliable, and user-friendly.
